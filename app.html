About PromptLens

PromptLens is an AI-powered assistant designed to enhance your browsing experience and boost your creativity and productivity.

Core Features:

  • Image Scraper: Analyze any image on the web and generate a detailed text-to-image prompt to help you understand its artistic style. Use custom templates to create prompts for ad campaigns, social media posts, and more.
  • Smart Tab Manager: Organize your open tabs with ease. Tabs are automatically grouped by domain, and you can expand, collapse, or close them directly from the sidebar.
  • AI Tab Summaries: Instantly get a concise, AI-generated summary of any open tab's content by clicking the âœ¨ icon.
  • "Find or Fetch" Search: Use the powerful semantic search to find what you're looking for. It searches through your open tabs first, and if it can't find a match, it suggests relevant new websites to explore.

This extension is open-source. For installation instructions, to report issues, or to contribute, please visit the official GitHub repository.
